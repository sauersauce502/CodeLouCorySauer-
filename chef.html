We choose our cooks the way that we chose our location; in respect to our roots and customers. In this philosophy we found our head chef and star of the show, Kristopher "WolfGang" Craven. Growing up only minutes from our storefront, Mr. Craven is very well-known in the neighborhood and amongst our customers. His one desire is to create the perfect meal for each hungry mouth; he starts with only the finest ingredients and creates everything from scratch with his own homemade recipes. He is a culinary master having studied at the Le Cordon Bleu academy, while also serving time under some of the most renowned chefs in Paris, France. Make a trip down to see us and I guarantee you'll see Chef Craven hard at work putting his heart and love for his community into each and every plate served.
