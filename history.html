From a simple idea that me and my father had concocted one Thursday night; while cooking some tailgate food before the start of a Philadelphia Eagles game, to the local staple that everyone in the Germantown area has come to know and love. We do not claim to be 5-star chefs and we most definitely are not a fine dining experience; but what we may lack in prestige we make up for with atmosphere; taste and brotherly love. Just like my beloved Eagles, we wanted to try and emulate the same energy that can be found in the very city of Philadelphia
